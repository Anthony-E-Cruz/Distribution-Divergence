Welcome To Distribution Divergence

This is a single page application developed to further my understanding of D3(Data-Driven Documents) and vanilla Javascript.

The three charts represented on this page give a visual depiction of the weath gap in the United States by dividing the population into four economic groups.

  • Top 1%
  • Top 90-99%
  • Top 50-90%
  • Bottom 50%%

The Dow Jones Industrial Average chart represents the performance of the stock market over the last 20 years to display the growth in overall wealth in the United States.

The Wealth Distribution chart represents the overall wealth held by each of the four economic groups over the last 20 years.

The Current Wealth Distribution Chart represents the current distribution of wealth across the four economic groups.
